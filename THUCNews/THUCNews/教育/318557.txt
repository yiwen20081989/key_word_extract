2月7日北京雅思考生口试改在9日进行
　　报考2009年2月7日北京市教育考试指导中心IELTS考试的所有考生的口语考试安排在2月9日（周一），口试场地仍为北京市教育考试指导中心1号楼。请提前两个工作日上网查询您的口试时间，并准时参加考试。
　　Monday Interview at Beijing Education Examinations Instructive Centre – Feb 09, 2009
　　The interview time for all candidates registered for the Feb 7th 2009 test at Beijing Education Examinations Instructive Centre will be arranged for Monday, Feb 9th 2009, and the venue remains to be the No.1 Building of Beijing Education Examinations Instructive Centre. Please check your interview time on the registration website 2 working days prior to the test.
