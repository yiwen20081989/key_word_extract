教育展参展高校简介：温哥华电影学校
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为加拿大国此次参展院校温哥华电影学校简介：
　　Vancouver Film School
　　温哥华电影学校
　　For over 20 years, VFS has provided a complete education to artists from around the world. Our 13 production-oriented programs span every aspect of the entertainment arts, and respected industry professionals guide students though a curriculum designed to deliver a comprehensive education in one year or less. VFS graduates include many of the filmmakers, animators, artists, writers, actors, designers, and talent managers who shape today’s global entertainment industry。
