教育展参展高校简介：德国柏林自由大学
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为德国此次参展院校德国柏林自由大学简介：
　　Freie Universität Berlin is a leading internationally oriented university which always ranks among the Top 10 universities in German national comparison and always achieves first position in terms of the number of placements among the top 10 universities in a range of rankings。
　　Freie Universität is also one of the largest universities in Germany, offering degree courses in about 100 subjects for 34,000 students。
　　Freie Universität has a literature stock of nearly nine million volumes and 22,000 ongoing magazine subscriptions. Additionally, there are extensive holdings in electronic media。
　　Additional offers are: the International Club, Programmes of the International Summer University (FUBiS)， as well as sports activities。
　　Freie Universität maintains wide-ranging international contacts to other universities and organizations which provide key impulses for research and teaching. In China Freie Universität Berlin maintains partnerships and cooperation agreements with the following universities:Peking University, Nanjing University, Fudan University, Sun Yat-Sen University, Lanzhou University, Nanjing Agricultural University and Chinese University of Hongkong。
