教育展参展高校简介：新布伦瑞克大学
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为加拿大国此次参展院校新布伦瑞克大学简介：
　　University of New Brunswick
　　新布伦瑞克大学
　　Established in 1785, the University of New Brunswick (UNB) provides undergraduate and graduate degree programs on two campuses in the cities of Fredericton and Saint John。
　　As the oldest English university in Canada, UNB has a proud history of educational excellence, attracting top students from over 100 countries each year. The largest university in the province of New Brunswick, UNB is, by reputation, one of the top five universities in Canada. Students may take advantage of our exciting co-op programs and Employment Centres to gain valuable Canadian work experience. UNB's degree programs, offering over 150 different specialties under 13 different faculties, are innovative and progressive. UNB offers dynamic degrees in the following subject areas: Arts, Business Administration, Science, Computer Science, Engineering, Education, Nursing, Environmental Studies, Forestry, Law, Health Sciences, and Sports Science. Students may be conditionally admitted to some undergraduate programs without submitting an English language test score. Conditionally accepted students are assessed on their English language skills when they arrive on campus.
