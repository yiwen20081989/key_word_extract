教育展参展高校简介：法国特鲁瓦高等商业学校
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为法国此次参展院校法国特鲁瓦高等商业学校简介：　　
　　ESC Troyes was established in 1992 and is the most recent Ecole Supérieure de Commerce (Graduate School of Management) in France. In 1999 it became the Groupe ESC Troyes with the development of new programs. Following a logical path of accreditation and recognition, Groupe ESC currently educates 1,100 students from baccalauréat to 5 years post baccalauréat level, with 4 higher education programs and with key values: pluridisciplinarity, tailor-made curricula, international experience for everyone, personal guidance for students and employability in the job market, in France and abroad。
　　Management, Tourism, International, Design and Applied Arts, Sports Management, Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Creation of a company, Executive Education, … Groupe ESC Troyes is positioning itself in the areas of expertise with high added-value。
