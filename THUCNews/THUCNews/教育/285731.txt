教育展参展高校简介：Study in Poland
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为波兰此次参展院校波兰Study in Poland简介：
　　Study in Poland
　　Poland has a very strong and well-developed system of higher education. Study in Poland represents country’s best universities offering studies in English in the fields of technology, science, medicine, business and humanities on BA, BSc, MA, MSc and PhD level. Having a large student population Poland prides itself on a lively cultural and social student life。
　　When you choose to study in one of our universities you will get a solid European higher education, and you will live in a safe and friendly country. Poland is one of the largest countries of the European Union, it has a vibrant economy but the cost of living is on affordable level and studying is not expensive。
　　Polish Pavilion at the China Education Expo 2009 has been set up by the Polish Agency of Enterprise Development and Perspektywy Education Foundation in preparation for participation of Poland at the World Expo Shanghai 2009.
