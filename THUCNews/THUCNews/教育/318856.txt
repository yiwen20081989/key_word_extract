　　SAYING　　MAYBE
　　When you aren’t sure of an answer or don’tway to say no directly, you might sayone of the following forms of maybe.In American society, it’s okay to initially say maybe to a request if you’re not sure whether you can (or want to)do it. But it’s considered very bad manners not to eventually say either yes or no. Here are some common expressions meaning maybe:
　　Possibly

　　There’sa chance
