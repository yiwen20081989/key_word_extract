参展高校简介：李光耀公共政策学院 新加坡国立大学
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为英国此次参展院校李光耀公共政策学院， 新加坡国立大学简介：
　　Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy, National University of Singapore
　　李光耀公共政策学院， 新加坡国立大学
　　The Lee Kuan Yew Schoolof Public Policy was formally established in 2004 as an autonomousgraduate school of the National University of Singapore. Its mission isto provide opportunities for the education and training of the nextgeneration of Asian policy-makers and leaders, with the objective ofraising the standards of governance throughout the region, improvingthe lives of the people in the region and, in so doing, also contributeto the transformation of Asia。
　　The School offers generousscholarships to outstanding candidates from anywhere in the world whocombine academic and professional achievement with an interest inpublic service. Currently, the School offers 4 Master and 1 PhDprogrammes。
　　李光耀公共政策学院于2004年正式成立，是新加坡国立大学属下的自主大专院校。学院的使命是为新一代亚洲政策制定者与领袖提供教育与培训机会，目的在于提高区域的管理水平，改善区域人民的生活，从而为转变中的亚洲作出贡献。
　　目前学院现有四项硕士课程。李光耀公共政策学院般发慷慨的奖学金给优秀的学生。
