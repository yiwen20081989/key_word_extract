教育展参展机构简介：巴兰丁佛
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为英国此次参展院校澳洲巴兰丁佛简介：
　　PARANINFO
　　巴兰丁佛
　　Paraninfo isa school in Madrid specialized in teaching Spanish for Foreigners.Thanks to its fine location, with easy access to public transport andfor its vast knowledge and experience gained (since 1973)， it has beena model in the formation of the language. Paraninfo school is an“Accredited Centre” of the Cervantes Institute which is responsible forspreading the Spanish language worldwide. The accreditation means thatthis school has passed quality control established by the saidInstitute. On completion of the course, students receive accrediteddiplomas. They can enjoy a healthy multi-cultural atmosphere. Highquality teaching and excellent language learning are offered。
　　Facilities are
　　- Spacious
　　- Well-situated
　　- Computer rooms are well equipped with the latest technology
　　- Free Internet access. (from 9h to 21h)
　　- New generation technology
　　- Personal assistance
　　- Pleasant atmosphere
　　巴兰丁佛是一所专业的对外西班牙语教育机构。它地理位置优越，交通条件便利且有三十多年来积累的丰富的教学经验，具备完整而规范的教学计划，它已经成为众多语言学校的典范。巴兰丁佛是由西班牙语教学的世界最权威的塞万提斯学院授权的，也就是说它是达到了塞万提斯学院所要求的标准。学生在学习期间除了可以获得相应的文凭以外，还可以享受到多国文化的熏陶与教育。其教学质量是完整而高等的。

　　- 全新教学设备
