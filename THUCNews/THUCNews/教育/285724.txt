参展高校：Federal Agency on Education
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为俄罗斯此次参展院校Federal Agency on Education简介：
　　Federal Agency on Education
　　俄罗斯联邦教育科学部和俄罗斯联邦教育总署
　　Federal Agency on Education is a Federal Agency of the Executive Authority。
　　Federal Agency on Education renders state services in the educational sphere and
　　development of public educational resources。
　　In a set order Federal Agency on Education holds tenders, makes state contracts on order
　　placement on goods delivery, work ful.llment, rendering services, realization of research,
　　experimental and technological works for state needs in the educational sphere。
　　Federal Agency on Education fulfils functions of State Customer of Federal Special
　　Programmes。
