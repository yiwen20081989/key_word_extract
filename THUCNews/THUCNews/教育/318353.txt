抢先看：9月27号雅思考试作文预测
　　一级预测(Highly Possible):政府决策类
　　入选理由: 作为最常考的两大类话题之一，自从今年8月16号之后，连续4次雅思考试都没考到该类话题，经过这段时间的盘整之后，政府类topic可望引来强势回归！
　　推荐练习真题: More and more security measures are being taken by the government to reduce crimesin urban areas. Some people believe such a practice is simply waste of money. What is your opinion?
　　二级预测(Possible): 青少年教育类
　　推荐练习真题: Some people believe that a teacher's only task is to teach the students academic subjects. Others argue that they should also be responsible for their moral education. Discuss the both viewpointsand give your own opinion.
　　三级预测(Maybe...): 意识形态类(抽象类)
　　入选理由: 同样是最近考的比较少的话题，可适度关注。
　　推荐练习真题: Some people believe that the study of history is of little use while others arguethat it helps us to know the present. Discuss the both viewpoints and give your own opinion.
