教育展参展高校简介：康奈尔理工学院
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为新西兰此次参展院校康奈尔理工学院简介：
　　Cornell Institute of Business and Technology
　　康奈尔理工学院
　　Central location Cornell Institute of Business and Technology (CIBT) is located in the city centre of Auckland, only a short distance from all the major tourist attractions and the commercial business centre of the city. All our facilities are ultramodern with spacious and learner-friendly classrooms and computer laboratories. Free access to the internet, email and the availability of the latest computers allow for an efficient learning experience in a diverse and modern teaching environment. All our staff are experienced in international education and are dedicated in their approach to continuous professional development and upgrading skills for the benefit of students. University of Cambridge International Examinations, one of the world’s leading providers of educational qualifications and assessments。
