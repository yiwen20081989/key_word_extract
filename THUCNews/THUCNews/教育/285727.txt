教育展参展机构简介：ISMET
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为英国此次参展院校澳洲ISMET ( Instituto Superior Medicinas Tradicionales)简介：
　　ISMET ( Instituto Superior Medicinas Tradicionales)
　　Ismet is aSpanish institute that specializes in the teaching and practices ofNatural Therapies. It was founded in 1992 and we are located inBarcelona. Our goal has always been to meet the needs of our studentsthrough interactive methodologies that enable the development of theircareers。
　　We provide an extensive range of courses in Naturapathy,Homeopathy, Osteopathy, Acupunture, and Traditional Chinese Medicine,Manual Techniques, Wellness and Natural esthetics, Personal growth andenergetic therapies。
　　We are looking for universities or institutesinterested in forming a partnership to exchange teachers, students, andresearch personnel。
