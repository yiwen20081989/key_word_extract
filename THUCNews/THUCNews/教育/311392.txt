同等学力考研英语单词周周练之四
　　1,break
　　vt. /vi.(broke，broken)①打破，折断，打碎：The boy broke the teapot.
　　②损坏：Be careful not to break the instruments,
　　③破坏，违反：break an agreement破坏协定, keep promise
　　④中断，打断，停止；break sb. 's sleep打扰某人的睡眠
　　n.暂停，休息，课间休息：Now let's have a break. Count time 
　　break away(from)突然离开，强行逃脱
　　breakdown损坏；分解，瓦解；(健康等)垮掉，崩溃
　　Negotiations broke down after only two days.
　　break in强行进人，闯人；打断，插嘴, 训练
　　Denis was quite new to the job so we are still breaking him in
　　break into非法闯人，强行进人
　　Someone broke into my car and stole the radio.
　　break off中断，突然停止
　　Annette broke off their engagement only a few weeks before they were due to marriage.
　　break out逃出，逃脱；突然出现，爆发
　　break through冲破，突围；取得突破性成就
　　Several demonstrators broke through the barriers despite warming from policemen. 
　　break up中止，结束；打碎，粉碎；散开，驱散
　　Police moved in to break up the meeting.
　　There are plans to break the company up into several small independent companies.
