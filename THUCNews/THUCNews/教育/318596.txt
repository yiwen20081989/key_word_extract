　　For the IELTS test in Guangzhou on 5 March 2009, the test venue for the written test will be changed to Information College of Zhongkai University of Agriculture and Engineering. The speaking test will be held at Zhongkai Hostel, 24 Dongsha Street, Fangzhi Road, Guangzhou。
