教育展参展高校简介：北英属哥伦比亚大学
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为加拿大国此次参展院校北英属哥伦比亚大学简介：
　　University of Northern British Columbia
　　北英属哥伦比亚大学
　　The University of Northern British Columbia is one of Canada’s newest public universities and has already achieved an excellent domestic and international reputation. In 2009, UNBC was ranked as the best small university in Western Canada and #2 out of 21 small universities in Canada by Maclean’s Magazine。
　　UNBC aspires to be Canada’s premier small, research-intensive university, with internationally recognized academic and research programs that prepare its graduates in select areas of high relevance to our region, province, and beyond. With a core campus in Prince George and regional campuses throughout northern BC, UNBC recognizes the importance of quality teaching, personal attention, and ground-breaking research。
　　Students seeking to meet UNBC English Language proficiency requirements for admission into Undergraduate program may do so through the English Language Studies program.
