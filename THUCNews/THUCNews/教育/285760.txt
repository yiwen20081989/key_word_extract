教育展参展高校简介：荷兰瓦格宁根大学
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为荷兰此次参展院校荷兰瓦格宁根大学简介：
　　Wageningen University--University of life sciences, food and environment
　　Wageningen University and Research Center (Wageningen UR) is a unique organisation that combines fundamental, strategic and applied research with innovative education at BSc, MSc and PhD levels. During recent decades Wageningen University has evolved into one of the world's leading centres and even number one in Europe in life sciences, food and environment. Its research and education are both international and internationally renowned, which attracts students from more than 100 countries all over the world。
　　Wageningen University is listed in the top 100 of all universities in the world and the language of instruction is English. Students and guests find in Wageningen a pleasant study environment and an opportunity for exchange of expertise.
