雅思77天倒计时基本功训练：各种时态综合
　　Part I：单词与短语
　　Nigeria 尼日利亚；England英格兰，英国；get a surprise(动词短语)大吃一惊；in the country在乡下；complete vt.完成；modern adj.现代派的；look strange看上去怪怪的；district n.区域，地区，行政区
　　Part II：语法学习
　　1.过去时、一般现在时、过去将来时、一般将来时、现在进行时、过去完成时、情态动词、条件从句、宾语从句、被动语态的复合运用
　　示例1：I had a letter from my sister yesterday. (有过去时)
　　示例2：She lives in Nigeria. (一般现在时)
　　示例3：She said that she would come to England next year.(有过去时，有过去将来时，有宾语从句)
　　示例4：If she comes, she will get a surprise. (条件句)
　　示例5：We are now living in a beautiful new house. (现在进行时)
　　示例6：Work on it had begun before my sister left.(过去完成时)
　　示例7：The house was completed five months ago. (被动语态)
　　示例8：I told her that she could stay with us.(有过去时，有宾语从句，有情态动词)
　　2.…, so…结构
　　so表示结果，使用的过程中请牢记这个结构的模式
　　示例：It is a very modern house, so it looks strange to some people. 
　　Part III：综合训练
　　Yesterday I had a letter from my sister (1) lives in Nigeria. (2) her letter, she said (3) she (4) come to England next year (5) if she (6 come), she will get a surprise, (7) we are now living in a beautiful new house (8) the country. The house was (9 complete) five months ago, (10) work (11) it (12) begun (13) my sister (14 leave). (15) my letter, I told her (16) she (17) stay with us, (18) the house has many large rooms (19) there is a lovely garden, too. It is such a modern house (20) it looks strange (21) some people. There are no (22) modern houses in the district, (23) it must be the (24) modern one.
　　答案：
　　(1) who; (2) In; (3) that; (4) would; (5) and; (6) comes; (7) for; (8) in; (9) completed; (10) but; (11) on; (12) had; (13) before; (14) left; (15) In; (16) that; (17) could; (18) for; (19) and; (20) that; (21) to; (22) other; (23) so; (24) only
　　Part IV：句型转换
　　将下面句子进行最大程度的合并：
　　1. Yesterday I had a letter from my sister. She lives in Nigeria. 
　　2. In her letter, she said that she would come to England next year. If she comes, she will get a surprise. We are now living in a beautiful new house in the country.
　　3. The house was completed five months ago. Work on it had begun before my sister left.
　　4. In my letter, I told her that she could stay with us. The house has many large rooms. There is a lovely garden, too.
　　5. It is such a modern house. It looks strange to some people. 
　　6. There are no other modern houses in the district. It must be the only modern house.
　　答案：
　　1. Yesterday I had a letter from my sister who lives in Nigeria.
　　2. In her letter, she said that she would come to England next year and if she comes, she will get a surprise, for we are now living in a beautiful new house in the country.
　　3. The house was completed five months ago, but work on it had begun before my sister left.
　　4. In my letter, I told her that she could stay with us, for the house has many large rooms and there is a lovely garden, too.
　　5. It is such a modern house that it looks strange to some people.
　　6. There are no other modern houses in the district, so it must be the only modern house. // There are no other modern houses in the district; therefore, it must be the only modern house.
