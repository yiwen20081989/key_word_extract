2010年硕士研究生考试英语小作文重点关注
　　申请信
　　1.       Directions:
　　You want to be a volunteer to service the Shanghai World Exposition. Write a letter to the department concerned to state why and how you will become a volunteer。
　　You should write about 100 words on ANSWER SHEET 2.
　　Do not sign your own name at the end of the letter. Use “Li Ming” instead。
　　Do not write the address。(10 points)
　　2.       Directions:
　　You will graduate from university and are hunting for a job. After reading a job ads, write a letter to Personnel Resources Department to:
　　1)      state your reason to write the application,
　　2)      describe your relevant education background,
　　3)      introduce your English level, and
　　4)      state your way of contact。
　　You should write about 100 words on ANSWER SHEET 2.
　　Do not sign your own name at the end of the letter. Use “Li Ming” instead。
　　Do not write the address。(10 points)
