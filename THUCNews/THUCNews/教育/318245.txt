　　这里没有新的语法内容，只有前面所学语法项目的混合运用。比如：状语从句、宾语从句、定语从句、介词＋动词的ing形式等。所以，就不再特意举例了。
　　Part III：综合训练
　　Nearly a week passed (1) the girl was able to explain what (2) happened to her. One afternoon she set (3) from the coast (4) a small boat (5) was (6 catch) in a storm. (7) evening, the boat (8 strike) a rock (9) the girl (10 jump) into the sea. Then she (11 swim) to the shore after (12 spend) the whole night (13) the water. (14) that time, she covered a distance (15) eight miles. Early next morning, she saw a light ahead (16) she knew she was near the shore (17) the light was high up (18) the cliffs. On (19 arrive) at the shore, the girl (20 struggle) (21) the cliff (22) the light she (23) seen. That was all she remembered. (24) she (25 wake) up a day later, she (26 find) herself (27) hospital.
　　答案：
　　(1) before; (2) what; (3) out; (4) in; (5) and; (6) caught; (7)towards; (8) struck; (9) and; (10) jumped; (11) swam; (12) spending; (13) in; (14) During; (15) of; (16) and; (17) because; (18) on; (19) arriving; (20) struggled; (21) up; (22) towards; (23) had; (24) When; (25) woke; (26) found; (27) in
　　Part IV：句型转换
　　将下面句子进行最大程度的合并：
　　1. Nearly a week passed. The girl was able to explain what had happened to her. 
　　2. One afternoon she set out from the coast in a small boat. She was caught in a storm.
　　3. Towards evening, the boat struck a rock. The girl jumped into the sea.
　　4. She jumped into the sea. She began to swim to the shore.
　　5. She reached the shore. She spent the whole night in the water.
　　6. During that time, she was worn out. She had covered a distance of eight miles.
　　7. Early next morning, she saw a light ahead. She knew she was near the shore. The light was high up on the cliffs. 
　　8. On arriving at the shore, the girl struggled up the cliff towards the light. She had seen the light.
　　9. She woke up a day later. She found herself in hospital.
　　答案：
　　1. Nearly a week passed before the girl was able to explain what had happened to her.
　　2. One afternoon she set out from the coast in a small boat and was caught in a storm.
　　3. Towards evening, the boat struck a rock and the girl jumped into the sea.
　　4. Jumping into the sea, she began to swim to the shore.
　　5. She reached the shore after spending the whole night in the water.
　　6. During that time, she was worn out, for she had covered a distance of eight miles.
　　7. Early next morning, she saw a light ahead and she knew she was near the shore because the light was high up on the cliffs. 
　　8. On arriving at the shore, the girl struggled up the cliff towards the light she had seen.
　　9. When she woke up a day later, she found herself in hospital.
