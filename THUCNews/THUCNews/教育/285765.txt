教育展参展高校简介：葡萄牙ISCTE
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为葡萄牙此次参展院校葡萄牙ISCTE简介：
　　BUSINESS SCHOOL - Lisbon University Institute (ISCTE)
　　The Business School of Lisbon University Institute (ISCTE) is one of the leading business schools in Portugal. It offers programmes at the Bachelor, Master and PhD level, lectured partly or completely in English。
　　It has corporate and academic partnerships in more than 50 countries and it jointly offers programmes with local universities in all continents of the world. The Business School of Lisbon University Institute has, e.g, partnerships in China, India, Angola, Mozambique, Thailand, Indonesia, Japan, USA and in many European countries. All these connections are at the disposal of our students and we actively promote contacts among our global network。
