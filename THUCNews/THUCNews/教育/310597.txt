同等学力申硕英语考试例题及答案精解
　　环球卓越同等学力英语例题精解
　　1. You will want two trees about ten feet apart, from ________to suspend your tent.
　　A there B then C which D where
　　2. That was so serious a matter that I had no choice but ______the police.
　　A called in B calling in C call in D to call in 
　　3. Americans eat ________as they actually need every day.
　　A twice as much protein B protein twice as much
　　C twice protein as much D protein as twice much
　　1. 正确答案：C
　　例题解析： 介词+关系代词+不定式作定语，只有which 是关系代词。
　　2. 正确答案：D
　　例题解析：Have no choice but 后要带接to 的不定式，意思是 只能……别无选择。 
　　3. 正确答案：A 
　　例题解析：译文：美洲人每天所食用的蛋白质是他们实际需要的两倍。倍数+as+形容词+as，表示倍数
　　4.The more we looked at the picture, ____________.
　　A the better we like it B we liked it less
　　C it looked better D the more we liked it 
　　5. His method of doing research work is hardly appreciated; he feels inferior________ others.
　　A than B as C with D to 
　　6. Their watch is ______to all the other watchers on the market.
　　A superior B super C beneficial D advantageous
　　4. 正确答案：D
　　例题解析：The +比较级，the+比较级，表示越……，越……。
　　5. 正确答案：D 
　　例题解析：译文：他的科研方法未得到赏识，他感到低人一等。
　　Inferior，superior，perfect本身含有比较意义，故表示比较时，其后不用连词that，而用介词to 指出比较对象。
　　6.正确答案：A 
　　例题解析：译文：他们的表是市场上最好的。
　　Superior 是优于……，后不用that，而用to为固定搭配
