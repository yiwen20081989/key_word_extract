教育展参展高校简介：一桥大学
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为日本国此次参展院校一桥大学简介：
　　Hitotsubashi University
　　一桥大学
　　Hitotsubashi University is an academic institution that primarily focuses on the social sciences and the study of civil society. The university has been contributing to the development of culture as well as the social economy in Japan with a traditionally liberal,lively academic climate.
　　Since the university's foundation, faculty and staff alike have endeavored to enhance standards of education and research. Hitotsubashi University has also been producing prominent individuals active in business and social circles in Japan and abroad, which we are proud of.
