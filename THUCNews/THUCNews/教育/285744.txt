教育展参展高校简介：德国九所理工大学
　　2009年中国国际教育展将于10月17日在北京拉开帷幕，随后巡回武汉、上海、成都和广州5个城市，以下为德国此次参展院校德国九所理工大学简介：
　　TU9 German Institutes of Technology: RWTH Aachen, TU Berlin, TU Braunschweig, TU Darmstadt, TU Dresden, Universität Hannover, Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)， TU München, Universität Stuttgart
　　TU9 is the network of the 9 leading technical universities in Germany. Their educational and research focus is on engineering and the sciences, areas in which they cooperate closely with national and international industries. TU9 universities are centres of scientific and technical innovation. They are linked with the most important national and international research institutions which maintain branch labs on or in the vicinity of their campuses。
　　TU9 universities are partners of prestigious institutes of higher education all over the world with which they have agreements on the mutual recognition of courses, credits and degrees in order to facilitate the international mobility of students and graduates.
