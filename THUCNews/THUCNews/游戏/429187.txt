首届中国原创页游可持续发展高峰论坛
　　——国内网页游戏在国外的生存现状
　　“首届中国原创网页游戏可持续发展高峰论坛”即将在2010年11月20日在成都假日酒店蜀都厅召开，此次峰会吸引了来自同行各大主流网页游戏厂商参会。甚至还吸引了国外同行者的青睐。 “国内网页游戏在国外的生存现状”至底是怎么样的呢？Mr Gideon将为我们揭秘。
　　Gideon Kory serves as a member of Lekool Inc. advisory board.  Mr. Kory brings many years of experience in investment banking and business development to Lekool. Mr. Kory has been a member of the board of directors  and Chair of the Audit Committee of Harbin's SOKO Fitness and Spa Group since 2008. Mr. Kory  is  Managing Director of Freedom Investors Corp., an investment bank and registered broker-dealer, since 2008.  From 2005 through  2008, Mr. Kory was Managing Director of Pacific Summit Capital, an investment bank and registered broker-dealer. From 2003 through 2005, Mr. Kory served as Software Senior Research Analyst and  in 2003 was ranked as by Zacks as #2 Software Analyst on Wall Street. Mr. Kory was also Vice President of Mergers and Acquisitions for Roth Capital Partners, an investment bank and registered broker-dealer.  Prior to his involvement in the securities industry, Mr. Kory spent 15 years in engineering and management working with government, commercial enterprises, and research institutions and as an independent consultant Mr. Kory is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA).  Mr. Kory holds a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (BSEE) from the Israel Institute of Technology - Technion. Mr. Kory is fluent in English, Hebrew and Russian。
